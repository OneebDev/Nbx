FAQ

Clear Answers for Your Construction Needs

How fast can I get an offer and close with NBX?

+

We typically provide offers within 24-48 hours after property evaluation. Our closing process is flexible and can be completed in as little as 7 days or on your preferred timeline.

Do I need to repair or clean my property before selling?*

+

No, you don't need to make any repairs or clean your property. We buy properties in as-is condition, handling all repairs, cleaning, and renovations after purchase.

What kinds of properties does NBX buy or work with?*

+

NBX works with all types of properties including residential, commercial, industrial, and special-purpose buildings. We specialize in complex construction projects across various sectors.

Can you help with inherited or probate properties?*

+

Yes, we have extensive experience with inherited and probate properties. Our team understands the legal complexities and can guide you through the entire process.

How do I see your off-market deals as an investor?*

+

Investors can join our exclusive network to receive off-market deal notifications. Contact our investor relations team to get access to our investment opportunities.

What happens after I accept an offer?*

+

After accepting an offer, we handle all paperwork, coordinate with title companies, and schedule the closing. You'll receive your payment in full at closing, with no hidden fees or commissions.
